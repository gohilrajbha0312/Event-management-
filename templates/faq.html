Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventFlow.

EventFlow is a premium event management platform that allows you to discover, create, and manage events of all kinds — from tech conferences to music festivals, business networking to wellness retreats. Our platform connects event organizers with attendees worldwide.
To create an event, sign up as an Organizer, then navigate to your Organizer Dashboard and click "Create Event." Fill in the event details including title, description, date, venue, ticket pricing, and capacity. Upload a banner image and publish — it's that simple!
Yes! Creating events on EventFlow is completely free. You can set your own ticket prices (including free events), and we only charge a small service fee on paid ticket transactions. There are no upfront costs or monthly subscriptions.
Browse our Events page, find an event you're interested in, and click "View Details." On the event page, click "Register Now." For free events, you'll be registered instantly. For paid events, you'll be taken to our secure checkout to complete your payment.
We accept payments via UPI only. You can pay using any UPI app such as GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or Amazon Pay. Simply enter your UPI ID (e.g. name@paytm, name@ybl, number@upi) at checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket details immediately after payment.
Refund policies vary by event and are set by the organizer. Generally, refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event start time. For cancelled events, full refunds are processed automatically. Contact the event organizer or our support team for specific refund requests.
All your tickets are available in your User Dashboard under "My Tickets." You can view event details, download receipts, and access your digital tickets from there. We also send a confirmation email with your ticket information after registration.
A User account lets you browse and register for events, manage your tickets, and update your profile. An Organizer account includes all User features plus the ability to create events, manage attendees, track revenue, and access analytics through the Organizer Dashboard.
As an organizer, your dashboard provides real-time analytics including total attendees, ticket sales, revenue reports, and attendee lists. You can view performance for each individual event and export data for your records.
You can reach our support team via the Contact page, by emailing support@eventflow.com, or by calling +91-278-242-1234 during business hours (Mon-Sat, 10AM-7PM IST). We aim to respond within 24 hours.
